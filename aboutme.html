About me

I enjoy puzzle games (mainly chess) and some video games. Some of my hobbies are listed down below!

Hobbies

Chess

Chess is a game that I enjoy playing as it involves a lot of thinking
and strategy. The rules are very simple, but the game itself has much
depth.

osu!

osu! is a popular rhythm game that I enjoy playing in my free time.
There are four different game modes in osu but the only one that I
play regularly is standard osu in which the player clicks and drags circles
to the beat of the song.

Valorant

Valorant is a team-based free-to-play tactical first-person-shooter game
developed by Riot Games. This is a game that I genuinely do not enjoy playing.
I have never played a game that has made me more upset, angry, and disappointed
at myself and others than any other game I have played. I will never willingly
play this game by myself. However, for the sake of having friends, I only play
this game whenever another friend asks me to play.
